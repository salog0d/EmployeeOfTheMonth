OUR HISTORY

Discover how Employee of The Month was born and its mission to revolutionize the workplace

The origin of the project

Employee of The Month was born as an innovative response to the challenges of motivation and recognition in business environments. Inspired by Rockwell Automation, our training partner, we decided to combine gamification principles with business tools to create a platform that transforms the work experience.

With the mindset of "learning through play", we developed an ecosystem that promotes both professional growth and entertainment, proving that productivity and fun can coexist in perfect harmony.

Technologies used

Django 5.2 Django REST Framework Unity Engine PostgreSQL TailwindCSS JavaScript Docker

ROCKWELL AUTOMATION

Our training partner and inspiration behind the project

Rockwell Automation

Rockwell Automation, Inc. is a global leader in industrial automation and digital transformation. Headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, it provides industrial automation and information solutions that help its clients be more productive and sustainable.

As the training partner of the Employee of The Month project, Rockwell Automation inspired us with its culture of innovation and operational excellence. Its focus on continuous improvement and talent development was key to creating a platform that truly drives employee performance and engagement.

The collaboration with Rockwell Automation allowed us to understand the real needs of modern companies and design a solution that combines cutting-edge technology with effective management methodologies.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Answers to the most common questions about Employee of The Month

What exactly is Employee of The Month?

Employee of The Month is a gamified platform for corporate environments that transforms employee performance management into an interactive and motivating experience. It combines video game elements with productivity tools to foster recognition, healthy competition, and professional development.

How does the scoring system work?

Users earn points through various activities, such as completing assigned tasks, participating in training, contributing to projects, receiving peer recognition, and playing mini-games within the platform. These points determine your level and ranking on the leaderboard.

What technologies were used to develop the platform?

The platform is primarily built with Django and Django REST Framework for the backend, providing a robust and secure API. Mini-games are developed with Unity, allowing for high-quality interactive experiences. The frontend uses TailwindCSS and JavaScript to create a modern and responsive interface.

How does it integrate with other enterprise systems?

Employee of The Month offers a full RESTful API that enables integration with various enterprise systems such as CRM, ERP, project management systems, and HR platforms. This allows for synchronization of performance data, goals, and achievements from different tools used in the organization.

What advantages does it offer over traditional systems?

Unlike traditional performance management systems, Employee of The Month leverages gamification to boost employee motivation and engagement. Studies show this approach can increase productivity by up to 40%, reduce employee turnover, and significantly improve job satisfaction.

How can I implement this platform in my company?

Implementation is simple and scalable. We offer both cloud and on-premise deployment options, with a guided onboarding process by our team. The platform adapts to companies of any size, from startups to large corporations, and can be customized to meet the specific needs of each organization.
